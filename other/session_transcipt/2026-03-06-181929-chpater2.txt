вҸә There are two real bugs and one ordering issue:

  1. set_xlim/set_ylim called after adjust_text вҖ” adjust_text uses the current
  axis bounds to constrain label placement. At the time it runs the limits are
  still matplotlib's auto-limits (likely 0вҖ“1 or wider), so labels can be pushed
  outside the final visible area.
  2. tight_layout() called after adjust_text вҖ” tight_layout shifts the axes,
  invalidating all the text positions adjust_text computed.
  3. No fig.canvas.draw() before adjust_text вҖ” adjust_text needs the figure to
  be rendered to measure text bounding boxes; without it, overlap detection is
  unreliable.

  Correct order: set limits вҶ’ set labels/ticks вҶ’ tight_layout вҶ’ canvas.draw вҶ’
  adjust_text вҶ’ savefig.

вқҜ Methodology                                                               
  To measure structural similarity between each pair of CPS repeating       
  units, I developed a path-based fingerprint similarity score. The score   
  calculates Jaccard similarity coefficient between finerprint set of CPS   
  repeating units. The approach combines the ordered sequential topology of 
   each repeating unit, capturing monosaccharide residue identity,          
  glycosidic bond types, and along with their relative positions.
